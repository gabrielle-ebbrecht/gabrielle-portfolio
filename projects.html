Projects

A collection of independent projects exploring machine learning applications and visualization design. Focused on my personal areas of interest, these projects help me explore new areas in ML and uncover insights through visual data storytelling.

  • Machine Learning: While my DS coursework has given me an excellent foundational knowledge of ML techniques, I aim to explore more specific applications of topics that had less coverage during my graduate studies, using personal interest as a springboard for learning.

  • Visualization: I'm a highly visual thinker who believes data should not only inform but also connect. I approach visualization projects with an emphasis on clarity, accessibility, and integrity—using color, scale, and design choices intentionally to highlight meaning rather than distract from it.

I am actively working to populate this section with more interesting personal projects. Check back soon!
