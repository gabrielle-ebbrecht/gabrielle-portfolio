About Me

I am based in New York with a background in business and data science. My business program at Fordham University began with emphasis on consulting and CSR, leading to my first internship in the ESG space at Estée Lauder.

Drawn to digital products and data wrangling, I later joined a SaaS product team at Cox Automotive and shifted my academic concentration to technology, eventually pursuing an M.S. in Data Science to dive deeper into the algorithmic side of data. Throughout my graduate program and beyond, I served as a research assistant in the Computer and Information Sciences department, exploring emerging topics in machine learning and cybersecurity (check out this work in my Publications section). Through paper writing and conference presentations, I have become a savvy technical communicator.

Most recently, I worked at Nasdaq on Metrio—a B2B SaaS platform for sustainability data management—which combined my interests in ESG, digital products, and data science. As a data scientist, I collaborated closely with product, engineering, and customer success teams, built data transformation pipelines, and conducted analyses to support client-facing initiatives. I also led several long-term projects from ideation to deployment, developing AI and NLP tools that streamlined product workflows. My work has strengthened my skills in project management, prompt engineering, API integration, cloud computing, algorithmic development, and iterative prototyping.
