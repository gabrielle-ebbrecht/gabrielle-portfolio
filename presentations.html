Presentations

If everything on a slide competes for attention, nothing wins!

I see presentations as another form of storytelling where design and data meet to help ideas stick. My aim is to communicate visually, not textually, using composition, color, and pacing to draw attention to what's most important—resulting in presentations that engage and leave a lasting impression.

Note that while all slide decks below are made by me, the work included can also be accredited to my research partners and colleagues named on the deck.
